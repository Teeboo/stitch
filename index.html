About stitch

The primary objective of the project is based on service learning that will result in a contribution to economic growth, employment creation, social and cultural development and income generation different communities. Students are required to collaborate from various disciplines and come up with the best solutions on how the secondary cooperative called “STITCH” can be established to become a reality. This project aims to promote Proudly South African products made by women who have been in this industry for more than 15 years but have not seen real growth and success. This is not an exhaustive wish list of what the students can do but it’s important for the success of the cooperative. This will help individuals within different communities to showcase their work and create jobs, the outcome of this is selling traditional attires throughout the world using the online store or mobile application.

16 cooperatives are participating in the project, they each need a branding package to improve their individual identity. (Honors students will help on this aspect with something professional.) STITCH will need an e-commerce and an app for ordering online or make any inquiries about their products and ask for a quotation.
